Kyiv is the capital of Ukraine, the largest city in Ukraine

Kyiv is the capital and largest city of Ukraine, one of the largest and oldest cities in Europe. Ukraine is divided into left and right banks. There are many museums and ancient places in Kyiv. Such as "Golden Gate", "Kiev-Pechersk Lavra" and "Andrew's Descent".

Lviv is the most beautiful city in Ukraine

According to the statistics "The most beautiful cities of Ukraine" Lviv takes the second place. There are many ancient castles and museums in Lviv. One of the castles "Zolochiivsky". Zolochiv Castle is a castle, a monument of history and culture of national importance in the city of Zolochiv, Lviv region, Ukraine. Zolochiv Castle Museum-Reserve is a department of the Lviv Art Gallery.
